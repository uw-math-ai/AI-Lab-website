The University of Washington Math AI Lab (ai.math.uw.edu) is a research and education organization focused on using AI for math, directed by Vasily Ilin and Jarod Alper.

Most of our projects have been Lean formalization projects, with about half hosted at github.com/orgs/uw-math-ai. We have taught four courses on Lean (one, two, three, four), with two more scheduled this academic year. We are supported by the UW eScience Institute, UW IT Research Computing, AWS, and Nebius.

We meet Mondays and Wednesdays from 4–5:30 pm in Denny Hall 303. See our Spring 2026 projects and GitHub page for code repositories. Use the dropdown above for projects from previous quarters.
